About me:

Hello. My name is ProFam / Harry and I am a 9 year old YouTuber. I first joined YouTube on April, 6th 2022 with my first channel, which is now known as ProFam 2. My first few videos were little stop-motion films using 2 pieces of paper and my dad's laptop. At this time, I had little to no editing skills, so that's why most of my videos weren't the greatest quality. As my channel started to grow, I decided to download Wondershare Filmora and watched some basic video editing tutorials. This improved my channel by a long way. I started getting slightly annoyed by the Filmora watermark and I thought I was doomed until a friend recommended Capcut to me. I started watching a lot of tutorials on how to use Capcut and my editing skills improved and improved all the way until I had surpassed over 600 subscribers. I would like to say a MASSIVE thank you to everybody that watches my videos and shows me all of this love and support, because without you guys, I wouldn't be here.
